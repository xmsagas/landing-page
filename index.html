The outer space

Outer space, commonly referred to simply as space, is the expanse that exists beyond Earth and its atmosphere and between celestial bodies.

Some random information.

Star clusters are large groups of stars held together by self-gravitation.
Earth's Moon is the only place beyond Earth where humans have set foot.
A nebula is a giant cloud of dust and gas in space.
A galaxy is a huge collection of gas, dust, and billions of stars and their solar systems, all held together by gravity.
